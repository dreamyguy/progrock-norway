About

Progressive Rock Norway is an initiative to list both emerging and already established Prog Rock artists from Norway, in a way that would allow users to conveniently follow (on Spotify) and/or like the Facebook Page of all featured artists.

Each artist is listed with their name, Spotify profile, Facebook Page, playlist presence & activity status.

It is delivered along with a curated Spotify playlist by Wallace Sidhrée, an artist that is also present in the Spotify playlist.
